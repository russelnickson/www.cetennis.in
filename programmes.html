Programmes

We provide a wide array for programs to suit every individual, be it a National Champion or a beginner with no knowledge of tennis.

Beginners

This program is tailor-made for young, passionate children, who are very new to tennis.

Intermediate

Players who have mastered the basics of tennis enroll themselves in the intermediate program to jump to the next level.

Advanced Program

This program is centered towards the professionals, who compete in big tournaments. It strengthens and solidifies tennis players' match play and address key issues like sound technique and cool temperament ahead of tournaments. Fitness levels are also very closely monitored.

Adult Program

We also offer training to adults, who are keen to relax and rejuvenate after a tough day’s work and pass on the tips to their little ones too.

Individual Program

Facilitating one-on-one training and interaction with the coach, this program builds a player’s character as much as it builds match-play. Coaches also employ video analysis to iron out the creases in the player’s game.
