Welcome

We cordially welcome you to the official website of CE Tennis. Competitive Edge, kick-started by Meenaskshi Sundaram has served as a breeding ground for tennis champions across all levels.

Besides offering professional tennis coaching, we also provide holiday and summer camps for kids that blend training with relaxation.

CE Tennis catalyses young players to turn their dreams into reality. It is an unparalleled tennis coaching centre that inspires them to Learn, Leap and Lead!

Residential Coaching Camps

This program is specifically intended for players, who are keen to take up tennis as a career. Comfortable accommodation and healthy food will be supplied to the budding stars. This camp incorporates all aspects of tennis-skill, technique, temperament, hand-eye coordination and fitness. Another salient feature of the residential camp is that equal amount of importance is placed on academics.
